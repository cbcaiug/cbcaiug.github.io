About the Creator

Hello! I'm Derrick Musamali, the creator and developer behind the AI Educational Assistant.

My Mission

As a passionate developer with a deep respect for the work educators do, I saw an opportunity to use the power of AI to solve a real-world problem. Teachers in Uganda are adapting to the new Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), which requires a significant amount of preparation and administrative work.

This tool was born from a simple idea: to give teachers back their most valuable resourceâ€”time. By automating the drafting of lesson plans, schemes of work, and assessment items, my goal is to free up educators to focus on what they do best: inspiring and teaching their students.

Why This Tool?

My mission is to create practical, high-quality EdTech tools that are:

  • Accessible to All: This tool is, and will always be, free for teachers.
  • Secure and Private: Your API keys and chat history are stored on your device, not on my servers. You are in full control of your data.
  • Built for Uganda: The prompts and assistants are specifically designed to align with the NCDC's CBC framework.
