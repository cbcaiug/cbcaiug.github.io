About CBC AI

What is CBC AI?

CBC AI is a suite of specialized AI assistants designed specifically for Ugandan educators adapting to the Competency-Based Curriculum. Built by a teacher for teachers, it helps you create NCDC-aligned content in minutes instead of hours.

Our AI Assistants

Item Writer

Generate scenario-based assessment items with structured scoring guides.

Lesson Planner

Create detailed NCDC-aligned lesson plans following official templates.

Scheme of Work

Develop structured schemes based on syllabus requirements.

AI Co-Teacher

Brainstorm ideas and get instant teaching advice.

Grading Assistant

Get feedback suggestions for student essays using your rubric.

Lesson Notes

Generate comprehensive lecture notes for any topic.

Why CBC AI?

  • Free for Teachers: 20 free downloads and 50 free messages per account. Use your own API key for unlimited access.
  • NCDC-Aligned: All prompts and templates follow Uganda's Competency-Based Curriculum framework.
  • Privacy First: Your chat history stays on your device. We only store your email and quota usage.
  • Built by a Teacher: Created by Derrick Musamali, a Biology and Chemistry teacher who understands your challenges.
  • No Installation: Works in your browser. Install as a PWA for offline access.

How It Works

1. Sign Up: Create a free account with your email
2. Choose Assistant: Select the tool you need
3. Generate Content: Describe what you need and let AI do the work
4. Export: Download as Word, PDF, or save to Google Docs

Technology

CBC AI uses advanced language models from Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, and Groq. You can switch between models based on your preference and API availability.
