Hello! I'm Derrick Musamali, a secondary school teacher of Biology and Chemistry, and a passionate tech enthusiast.

My Mission

As a fellow educator, I know firsthand the challenges of adapting to the new Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). The amount of preparation and administrative work can be overwhelming, taking time away from our core mission: teaching students.

Instead of waiting for the perfect tool to appear, I decided to see if I could build it myself. Using the power of AI, I created this Educational Assistant to solve the very problems I was facing. My goal is simple: to give teachers back their most valuable resourceâ€”time.

Why This Tool?

My mission is to create practical, high-quality EdTech tools that are:

  • Accessible to All: This tool is, and will always be, free for teachers.
  • Secure and Private: Your API keys and chat history are stored on your device, not on my servers. You are in full control of your data.
  • Built for Uganda: The prompts and assistants are specifically designed to align with the NCDC's CBC framework.
