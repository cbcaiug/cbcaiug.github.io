Thank you for using the AI Educational Assistant ("the Service"). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled by our Service and why.

1. Information Stored On Your Device (Local Storage)

Your browser's local storage is used to save the following:

  • API Keys (Optional): If you provide your own API keys from third-party AI providers, they are stored only in your browser. They are never sent to our servers.
  • Chat History: Your conversations are saved in your browser so you can continue your work between sessions.
  • Application Settings: Your preferences, such as the selected AI model, are saved for your convenience.

2. Information Stored in Cloud Firestore

We use Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore to manage user accounts and quotas:

  • User Authentication: When you sign up, Firebase stores your email address and authentication credentials securely.
  • User Quotas: We store your download quota (20 free), message quota (50 free), and last active timestamp in Cloud Firestore. This data syncs across all your devices in real-time.
  • Activity Tracking: We track when you last used the service (timestamp only) to monitor active users. No chat content or personal messages are stored in Firestore.

3. Information Processed By Our Server

Certain features require processing data on our backend server (a secure Google Apps Script).

  • Generated Document Handling: When you use the "Save as Google Doc" feature, the AI-generated text is sent to our server to create a Google Document. These documents are stored in our private Google Drive folder. We may review these documents to analyze how the AI is performing and to improve the quality of our prompts and assistants. No personal information is attached to these documents.
  • Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (like which assistant is used and your browser type) through Google Analytics to understand how the Service is being used and how to improve it. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to personally identify you.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with third-party services whose own privacy policies apply:

  • AI Providers (e.g., Google, OpenAI): When you send a message, your prompt and API key are sent directly from your browser to the AI service you have selected.
  • Google Drive/Docs: The "Save as Google Doc" feature uses the Google Drive API to create and store documents as described in Section 2.

5. Data Security

Your chat history and optional API keys are stored locally on your device. Your account data (email, quotas) is stored securely in Cloud Firestore with access restricted by Firebase Security Rules. We recommend signing out if you are using a shared or public computer.

6. Children's Privacy

This Service is intended for educators and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us
