When your free quota is exhausted, you can add your own API key in Settings to continue using the service. API keys from providers like Google AI Studio are free to obtain and give you unlimited usage.

Yes! Your account syncs across all devices. Sign in with the same email to access your quota from any phone, tablet, or computer.

üîí Privacy & Security

Do you see my personal chats?

No. Your chat history is stored locally in your browser, not on our servers. We only store your email, quota usage, and last active timestamp for account management.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in and Cloud Firestore with security rules that prevent unauthorized access. Your API keys are stored only in your browser's local storage.

What happens to documents I save to Google Docs?

Documents exported using "Save as Google Doc" are stored in our system for quality improvement purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
